# Operations Portfolio

_*All projects listed were delivered within corporate enterprise environments; technical source code is proprietary and cannot be shared._

This domain focuses on delivering data solutions that optimize business processes, improve process throughput, and drive departmental KPI visibility.

### 1. Passenger Flow & Security Gate Optimization | Aviation
* Architected a predictive analytics pipeline to forecast passenger throughput, integrating flight schedules and seasonal demand to transition from static staffing to dynamic resource allocation.
* Engineered a distribution model that calculated congestion risk based on flight-specific load factors, enabling proactive terminal management and reduced wait times.
* Delivered a high-performance BI product that empowered stakeholders to simulate "what-if" scenarios, directly improving throughput efficiency and the overall passenger experience.
