### 1. Global CRM Intelligence

<p style="margin-top:-8px;"><strong><em>Sales Analytics | Industrial & Logistics | Wolvertem, Belgium</em></strong></p>

* **Context:**
The organization utilized Microsoft Dynamics AX to support global sales operations, but the underlying data was not being fully leveraged for management reporting. Leadership lacked clear visibility into pipeline health, win/loss trends, forecast accuracy, and comparative regional performance.

* **Stakeholders:**
Director of Sales, Regional Sales Teams, Executive Management, Business Analysts, and CRM System Stakeholders.

* **Approach:**
Designed and deployed a BI reporting suite transforming Dynamics AX data into a structured sales performance ecosystem.

* **Execution:**
  * Built reporting data model capturing end-to-end sales lifecycle
  * Automated pipeline analytics and stage progression tracking
  * Developed Target vs Actual reporting across regions
  * Created regional benchmarking dashboards
  * Built data quality reporting for CRM records
  * Aligned KPIs with business stakeholders

* **Result:**
Delivered automated global pipeline visibility, replacing spreadsheets and improving forecast monitoring and decision-making.

### 2. Performance & Business Management

<p style="margin-top:-8px;"><strong><em>Human Resources | Investment Banking | London, UK</em></strong></p>

* **Context:**
HR decision-making relied on manual reporting from PeopleSoft and Fieldglass, limiting strategic workforce planning.

* **Stakeholders:**
HR Executives, Workforce Planning Teams, Business Analysts, HR Systems Teams.

* **Approach:**
Modernized HR analytics through centralized automated BI platform.

* **Execution:**
  * Built automated pipelines integrating PeopleSoft & Fieldglass
  * Developed dashboards for workforce metrics (Joiners, Movers, Leavers)
  * Enabled cost modeling and workforce planning analytics
  * Integrated NPrinting for automated reporting decks
  * Standardized executive reporting processes
  * Conducted stakeholder demos for adoption

* **Result:**
Saved ~10 executive hours/month, improved reporting accuracy, and drove data-driven workforce planning.
